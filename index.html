ABOUT Domino Party Entertaiment

We can't wait to Party with You!

Domino Party Entertainment has been serving the NYC area for over 20 years. Hector Rosario started his DJ career at the age of seven. He would take his mother's records, and try to scratch with them. With the record "Roxanne Roxanne", he would literally scratch the record to the point where his mother would have to buy a new one. So his love of music started at a very young age. He started working for a DJ company at 14 years old where he learned what it took to throw successful Sweet 16s, Communions, weddings, and much more.

After some time, he thought, "Why isn't there a company that offers everything? So at 25 years old, he started "Domino Party Entertainment". He went out to find the best caterers, photographers, lighting companies, decor companies, florists, etc on Long Island. His goal was to be the premier one-stop-shop for entertainment in the area.

What Domino Party Entertainment is best known for is treating all of their clients like family. It's always business of course, but it's a party, so their motto is "Lets Have Fun"! They make sure that they cater to all of their clients' needs and make it a day they will never forget! They also know everyone has a budget, but also wants the best. Some people think this is may not be a reality, they make their clients' goals tangible. They work with any reasonable budget and make sure that their clients and their guests have an unforgettable event!
